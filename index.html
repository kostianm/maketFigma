About Olga

I was born in Kiev, Ukraine and I was very active child – I love sports. At the age of 13 I joined fitness class.I quickly became interested learning everything I could about nutrition. I tested many diets, especially those that were scientifically proven. I should say it was not so easy for me to change my food habits completely. I was practicing yoga for several years. I even tried vegetarian diet. I learned to stay disciplined, determined and goal minded. As a result of my determination, felt healthier, energized, happy and inspired to reach higher goals and standards for myself. In 2006 I began lifting serious weights and with assistance of yoga, and nutritionally rich meals, I noticed how my body began to change fast. I concentrated on better understanding how protein functions in the body and the effects of proper diet application. I became an expert. Despite challenges in live, such us destroyed marriage followed by depression and other general complications associated with living in poor country, I remained motivated and goal oriented. Workouts at the gym kept me focused on my goals.
